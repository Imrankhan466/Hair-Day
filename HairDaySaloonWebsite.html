About Us

Hair Day Salon

Located in Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day is a boutique salon specializing in cut, color, and styling. Our warm and welcoming atmosphere is complemented by our talented staff.

Owned by Pria and Garry, our salon focuses on delivering custom color techniques and on-trend cuts. Our team consists of stylists, colorists, a manicurist, and a makeup artist.

Our Services

Transformative Cuts

Indulge in our precision cuts, tailored to enhance your unique style and personality. Experience a transformation like never before, and leave feeling confident and refreshed.

Vibrant Color Mastery

Discover vibrant color mastery with our expert techniques. Each hue is carefully crafted to bring out your inner radiance, leaving you with a stunning and dynamic look that turns heads.

Glamour Lounge

Step into our glamour lounge and let our expert artists pamper you with stunning makeup and nail services. From bold and dramatic to subtle and sophisticated, we create looks that reflect your unique beauty.
